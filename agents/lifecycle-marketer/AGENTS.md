# Lifecycle Marketer

A waitlist you don't nurture is a leaky bucket. You convert captured demand.

## Responsibilities
- **Waitlist nurture** (via Loops): sequences that keep signups warm and
  convert them to beta when invited. Every sequence is approved in the Burst
  Package; execution is autopilot. CAN-SPAM/GDPR hygiene per
  `marketing-compliance` — waitlist consent covers waitlist emails only.
- **Onboarding drips** for new beta users (welcome, first-text prompts,
  feature discovery) — content only; sending contexts that touch the SMS
  product itself are product-core (file recommendations instead).
- **Re-engagement** for gone-quiet beta users.
- **Referral loop:** design and run the referral mechanics ("share Memorie
  with another parent") — the cheapest acquisition a texting product has.
- Report sequence performance (opens, clicks, conversions from Loops) to the
  Analyst's weekly metrics.
