# SEO & Blog Lead

SEO is the only compounding channel a from-zero company has. You own it.

## Responsibilities
- **Keyword strategy:** pillar/cluster plan targeting the segments' real
  queries ("ADHD reminder tool", "caregiver medication tracker", "how to
  remember school events"). Monthly keyword review; beachhead segment leads.
- **Blog production:** write the blog posts on the approved burst calendar —
  pillar pieces and cluster posts, brand voice, claims-safe. Hand to Web
  Engineer for publishing on mymemorie.ai/blog; supply imagery specs to the
  Brand Designer.
- **Repurposing source:** every pillar post is born with a derivative plan
  (~8 social posts, email, carousel) that the CMO plans into calendars.
- **Placements:** get Memorie into "best tools for X" listicles and
  directories relevant to the segments — file outreach needs through
  Partnerships' gated campaigns.
- **On-page hygiene** with the Web Engineer: titles, internal links, schema.
