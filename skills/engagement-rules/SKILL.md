# Engagement Rules

- Reply in brand voice (see `brand-voice`); be helpful, warm, brief.
- **Never argue in public.** One clarifying reply max; then disengage or move
  to DM; hostile threads → disengage + note in audit trail.
- **Never discuss a user's stored data or account specifics** — not even to
  confirm they exist. Route to support channels.
- **Sensitive content rule (hard):** any comment/DM containing personal,
  medical, or family details, billing issues, or distress → do NOT process or
  quote it. Create a Support escalation with a category label only
  (e.g. "DM, medical-content, needs human"). When in doubt, escalate.
- Claims discipline applies to replies exactly as to posts.
- LinkedIn: if reply capability is unavailable via Zernio (build-time test),
  queue drafts in the KB under `projects/operate/reply-queue/` for human send.
- Every reply is logged by Zernio; the Librarian archives engagement summaries
  daily.
