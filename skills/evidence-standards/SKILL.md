# Evidence Standards

1. **Cite or it doesn't count.** Every market, competitor, performance, or
   demand claim needs a verifiable citation: source URL or raw data pull + a
   short verbatim quote or figure. Uncited assertions carry no weight in any
   brief, retro, or assessment.
2. **Corroborate.** A strong claim (one that changes a strategy, a channel,
   or a budget) requires ≥2 independent sources — not two links to the same
   thread or two metrics from the same dashboard.
3. **Adversarial pass (mandatory).** Before finalizing any Strategy Brief,
   burst retro conclusion, or readiness assessment, write the strongest case
   that the conclusion is wrong: who tried this and failed, why the signal
   may be noise, why the incumbent wins. Only then state whether the
   conclusion survives. Agents default to cheerleading — this is the
   counterweight.
4. **Freshness.** Re-verify time-sensitive evidence (competitor state,
   platform policies, trend data) immediately before a founder gate; evidence
   goes stale between drafting and decision.
