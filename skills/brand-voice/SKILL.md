# Memorie Brand Voice

Conversational, empathetic, calm. We sell **relief from cognitive burden**,
never productivity hustle. Tagline register: "Text it and forget it." /
"Memorie removes complexity. Don't organize. Don't remember."

**Do:** plain words; second person; short sentences; acknowledge the mental
load ("the school forms, the med schedule, the birthday next Tuesday");
gentle humor; specific tiny moments over grand promises.
**Don't:** productivity-bro tone, urgency/FOMO, guilt ("stop failing your
family"), jargon, exclamation stacking, em-dash-heavy AI cadence, health
claims (see `claims-discipline`).

Segment registers: parents — warm, in-the-trenches; ADHD/executive function —
respectful, zero condescension, never clinical; caregivers — steady,
dignified, extra care (they carry heavy things).
